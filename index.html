About Me

Hello! My name is Alison, and I'm a third year student at UC Berkeley studying computer science. As both an artist and programmer, I'm passionate about the intersection of technology and design and am committed to exploring this through my work. My current interests include computational photography, computer graphics, and web design & development. In addition, I am also enthusiastic about ethics and the social implications of computer technology.

As a hobby, I enjoy learning how to create art in different mediums, such as graphic design, photography, sculpture, and video editing (feel free to check out some of my creative endeavors here!). Aside from creating, I love exploring new environments and experiences. Whether I'm traveling, visiting art museums, or hiking, I'm constantly seeking out new sources of inspiration.
