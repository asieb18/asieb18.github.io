About Me

Hello, my name is Alison. I'm a UC Berkeley alumni with a Bachelor's degree in computer science. As both an artist and programmer, I'm passionate about the intersection of technology and design. My main interests include computational photography, computer graphics, and web design and development. I am also enthusiastic about ethics and the social implications of computer technology.

As a hobby, I enjoy learning how to create art in different mediums, including graphic design, photography, video editing, and crochet. Aside from creating, I'm constantly searching for new sources of inspiration. I love visiting art museums, hiking, and discovering new cafes.
