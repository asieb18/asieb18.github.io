About Me

Hello! My name is Alison, and I'm a third year at UC Berkeley studying computer science. As both an artist and programmer, I'm passionate about the intersection of technology and design and am committed to exploring this through my work. My current interests include computational photography, computer graphics, and web design & development. I am also enthusiastic about ethics and the social implications of computer technology.

As a hobby, I enjoy learning how to create art in different mediums, such as graphic design, photography, sculpture, and crochet. Aside from creating, I love exploring new environments and experiences. Whether traveling, visiting art museums, or hiking, I'm constantly seeking out new sources of inspiration.
